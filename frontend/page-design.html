Analysis

Inflation peaked at 7.8% in December 2022 — the highest level since 1990 — driven by post-pandemic supply disruptions and strong domestic demand. The RBA responded with 13 consecutive rate increases, lifting the cash rate from a historic low of 0.1% to 4.35%. Annual CPI has since eased to 2.4%, returning within the target band for the first time in three years. Real wages are beginning to recover as wage growth now outpaces inflation.
